  \vfill
  \begin{abstract}
  We develop a localized trace formula on finite-area hyperbolic surfaces with cusps. 
  A microlocal projector onto energies in $[\lambda-\eta,\lambda+\eta]$ yields a geometric expansion supported on closed geodesics of length $\le T\asymp \log\lambda$, together with explicit, uniform-in-$Y$ remainder terms. 
  The construction provides a power-saving error for the identity and parabolic contributions, and leads to quantitative local Weyl laws and variance bounds for automorphic forms.
  \end{abstract}
