  \small
  \noindent
  \textbf{Abstract.}
  We develop a localized trace formula for finite-area hyperbolic surfaces
  $\Gamma\backslash\mathbb{H}$ with cusps. Using microlocal cutoffs and smooth
  spectral projectors, we establish a quantitative trace identity in which the
  spectral side is matched to a controlled geometric expansion over closed
  geodesics, with explicit remainder terms stable under refinement.
  Applications include a quantitative local Weyl law and modular trace estimates
  suitable for further analytic use.
  \end{tcolorbox}

  \vfill

  {\small Version: \today\par}
